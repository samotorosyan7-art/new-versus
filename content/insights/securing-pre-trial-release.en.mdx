## The Context

A client was detained pending trial following accusations of severe economic misconduct. The prosecution submitted a motion for pre-trial arrest, specifically petitioning for the client to be held at the Nubarashen penitentiary, citing flight risk.

## The Versus Strategy

During the high-stakes detention hearing, we dismantled the prosecution's argument regarding flight risk point-by-point. We presented a comprehensive dossier of the client's localized ties to Yerevan, business obligations, and lack of criminal history.

## The Outcome

The presiding judge rejected the prosecutor's petition for detention. We successfully secured immediate release under house arrest coupled with a reasonable bail amount, allowing the client to return to their family while awaiting trial.
