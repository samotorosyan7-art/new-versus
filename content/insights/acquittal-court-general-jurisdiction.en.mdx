## The Context

A client faced serious, fabricated assault charges relying heavily on matching testimonies from questionable witnesses. The trial took place at the Court of General Jurisdiction of Yerevan, with the prosecution seeking a multi-year prison sentence.

## The Versus Strategy

The defense focused entirely on inconsistencies within the investigative phase. During the trial, rigorous and relentless cross-examination exposed critical contradictions between the "eyewitness" statements and the timeline of events. Furthermore, we successfully motioned to suppress key physical evidence obtained through procedural violations.

## The Outcome

The court recognized the absence of a reliable evidentiary base. The client was fully acquitted of all charges and immediately exonerated.
