## The Context

The owner of a prominent logistics company headquartered in the Kentron administrative district was targeted by investigative authorities under serious allegations of financial fraud and systematic tax evasion. A conviction threatened the entire enterprise and carried severe custodial time.

## The Versus Strategy

We immediately intervened during the preliminary investigation phase. Instead of waiting for trial, we launched a proactive, independent forensic audit of the company's financials.

## The Outcome

We presented incontrovertible accounting evidence to the prosecutor's office, aggressively challenging the investigator’s initial premise. The financial crime charges were completely dismissed before an indictment was even formalized, saving the client's business and freedom.
